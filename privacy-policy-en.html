Privacy Policy – WordQuest

Last Updated: June 15, 2026

Welcome to WordQuest. This Privacy Policy explains how information may be collected, used, stored, and shared when you use the WordQuest mobile game ("WordQuest", "the App", "we", "us", or "our").

By downloading, accessing, or using WordQuest, you acknowledge that your information may be processed as described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

WordQuest does not require users to create a traditional account and does not request personal information such as names, postal addresses, or phone numbers.

However, certain information may be collected automatically through the App and the services it uses, including:

  • Device type and device model
  • Operating system and version
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Approximate geographic region based on IP address
  • Anonymous authentication identifiers
  • Gameplay progress and statistics
  • App usage information
  • In-app purchase transaction information provided by platform stores

This information is used to operate, maintain, improve, secure, and monetize the App.

2. Legal Bases for Processing

Where required by applicable law, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), WordQuest processes information on one or more of the following legal bases:

  • Your consent, where consent is required.
  • The performance of services requested by you through the App.
  • Our legitimate interests in operating, maintaining, securing, improving, and monetizing the App.
  • Compliance with applicable legal obligations.

3. Anonymous Authentication

WordQuest uses Unity Authentication with anonymous sign-in functionality.

When you use the App, an anonymous identifier may be created to support game services and cloud-based features. This identifier is not intended to directly identify you as an individual and does not require you to provide your name, email address, or other directly identifying information.

4. Game Progress and Cloud Save

WordQuest uses Unity Cloud Save to back up and restore certain gameplay-related information.

Data that may be stored includes:

  • Game progress
  • Daily Quest progress and results
  • Level Quest progression
  • Completed levels
  • Virtual currency balances (Droplets)
  • One-time rewards and achievements
  • Game settings and preferences
  • Language preferences

Cloud Save is intended to help preserve gameplay data. While reasonable efforts are made to maintain saved data, WordQuest does not guarantee that all progress can always be restored.

5. Analytics

WordQuest uses Unity Analytics to better understand how players interact with the App and to improve gameplay, performance, stability, and user experience.

Analytics data may include:

  • Gameplay events
  • Session information
  • Feature usage
  • Progression statistics
  • General usage patterns

Analytics information is used in an aggregated and anonymized manner whenever possible and is not intended to directly identify individual users.

6. Advertising

WordQuest may display advertisements, including:

  • Banner advertisements
  • Rewarded advertisements

Advertising services may be provided through Unity LevelPlay and related advertising partners.

These services may process information such as:

  • Advertising identifiers
  • Device information
  • Operating system information
  • Approximate location information
  • Ad interaction information

This information may be used to provide, measure, improve, and personalize advertising where permitted by law.

7. Consent and Privacy Choices

WordQuest uses Google User Messaging Platform (UMP) to manage advertising consent in regions where consent is required by applicable laws.

Depending on your location, you may be asked to:

  • Consent to personalized advertising
  • Reject personalized advertising
  • Manage privacy preferences

Users may review or update available advertising consent choices through the Privacy Choices option within the App, where supported.

8. In-App Purchases

WordQuest offers optional in-app purchases, including virtual in-game currency ("Droplets").

All purchases are processed by the applicable platform provider, including:

  • Apple App Store
  • Google Play Store

WordQuest does not collect, receive, or store payment card information, billing details, or other financial account information.

Purchase transactions are governed by the policies and terms of the applicable platform provider.

9. Third-Party Services

WordQuest may use third-party services that process information according to their own privacy policies.

These services may include:

  • Unity Authentication
  • Unity Cloud Save
  • Unity Analytics
  • Unity LevelPlay
  • Google User Messaging Platform (UMP)
  • Apple App Store
  • Google Play Store

These third parties may collect and process information independently in accordance with their own policies.

10. International Data Transfers

Some information may be processed or stored in countries outside your country of residence, including countries outside the European Economic Area (EEA).

Where required by applicable law, reasonable safeguards are used by our service providers to protect personal data transferred internationally.

11. Children's Privacy

WordQuest is intended for a general audience and is not specifically directed at children under the age of 13 or the minimum age required by applicable local privacy laws.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

If you believe information has been collected from a child in a manner that violates applicable law, please contact us so appropriate action can be taken.

12. Data Retention

Information may be retained only for as long as reasonably necessary to:

  • Provide game functionality
  • Maintain cloud save services
  • Support analytics and operational needs
  • Prevent fraud, abuse, or misuse
  • Comply with legal obligations

Aggregated or anonymized information may be retained for longer periods where permitted by law.

13. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location and applicable law, you may have certain rights regarding your personal data, including:

  • The right to access information
  • The right to request correction of inaccurate information
  • The right to request deletion of information
  • The right to restrict certain processing
  • The right to object to certain processing
  • The right to data portability where applicable
  • The right to withdraw consent where consent is the legal basis for processing

You may request deletion of cloud-stored gameplay data by contacting us. We may require sufficient information to identify the relevant game account or device before processing such requests.

We aim to respond to privacy-related requests within 30 days, where required by applicable law.

14. Data Security

Reasonable technical and organizational measures are used to help protect information against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

However, no method of electronic transmission or storage can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

15. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.

When changes are made, the "Last Updated" date at the top of this document will be revised.

Continued use of WordQuest after changes become effective constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
